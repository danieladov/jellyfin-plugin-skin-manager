    How to enable bluring of background fo dialogs and episode previes for Firefox

    Steps


    • go to about:config in firefox and click on "Accept the Risk and Continue".
    • Search for layout.css.backdrop-filter.enabled and change false to true.
    • Search for gfx.webrender.all and change it to true. Done!
